Want To Feel Younger When You're Older?

The Sun WILL Destroy Your Skin

Staying in the sun without protection will cause:

    Skin and Eyelid Cancer
    Cataracts
    Benign Tumors
    Wrinked, Creased Skin
    Discolored, Tough Skin

These changes may only start appearing later in life, work now to prevent them later.

Protect yourself from the sun using regular application of suncreen of SPF 30 or higher. Wear a hat and sunglasses if possible, and UV protective clothing. Priorizite protection on days when the UV index is high, or from 10am-2pm, when the sun is highest.
